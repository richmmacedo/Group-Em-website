Group' Em and Order

Home Learnings

Simplify and save on your food orderings today!

Group' Em and Order offers users incredible flexibility in grouping order with other people around them!
The features Group' Em offers make it easy to save on fees and tips without external communication.

In locations such as corporate offices, schools, and other large groups, Group' Em and Order can save you time and money.

Even when there are no active orders in the app you can create them yourself and allow others to join in later. This presents a great solution to looking for someone to split the cost of ordering out!

Through a centralized home screen, users can decide what path they will take within the app: joining others, creating orders for the public or even skipping everything and just ordering for themselves, whenever they feel like.
